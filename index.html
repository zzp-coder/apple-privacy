🔒 Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's exactly what we do (and don't do) with your data.

📄 QuickInvoice AI ⏱ Focus Timer Pro 🧾 Tip & Bill Split 🌱 Habit Tracker Pro 📐 Unit Converter Pro 🔑 Password Generator Pro
The Short Version: All your data stays on your device. We don't collect it, we don't transmit it, we don't see it. Ever.

1. Data We Collect

We collect nothing. Our apps do not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or any other data from you.

2. Data Storage

All data you create within our apps (invoices, habits, passwords, timer history, etc.) is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's standard local storage (AsyncStorage / NSUserDefaults).

  • Data never leaves your device
  • We have no servers receiving your data
  • Data is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Data is included in Apple's standard iCloud backup if you have it enabled

3. Network Access

Our apps do not make any network requests. They function completely offline. No data is ever transmitted over the internet.

4. Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking services.

5. Permissions

Some apps request the following device permissions, used only as described:

  • Notifications (Focus Timer Pro, Habit Tracker Pro): To send you timer completion alerts and daily habit reminders. You can disable this at any time in iOS Settings.
  • Clipboard (Password Generator Pro): To copy generated passwords to your clipboard when you tap "Copy".

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. As we collect no data at all, there is nothing to be concerned about regarding children's privacy.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last Updated" date below. Since we collect no data, any changes would only be to clarify our existing practices.
